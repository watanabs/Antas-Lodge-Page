Discover the Island

Explore Itbayat

Itbayat is the northernmost and most isolated municipality of the Philippines. Its ancient volcanic landscape, dramatic cliffs, and serene grasslands are unlike anywhere else on earth.

Rolling Grasslands

Trek across Itbayat's endless green hills that stretch toward the Pacific horizon — a landscape unchanged for centuries.

Dramatic Cliffs

Stand at towering cliffs where the island plunges into the deep Pacific.

Ivatan Stone Houses

Historic limestone and cogon-thatched homes built to withstand typhoons.

Villages

Walk quiet stone-paved paths through centuries-old Ivatan communities.

Breathtaking Sunsets

Watch the sun sink below the Pacific, painting the sky in amber and gold.
