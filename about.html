Our Story

A Family Haven
at the Edge of the World

Antas Family Lodge is a warm, welcoming rest house nestled in Itbayat — the remotest inhabited island of the Philippines. We offer genuine Ivatan hospitality to travellers seeking the extraordinary beauty of Batanes.

🏡
Ivatan Architecture

Built in the tradition of Batanes homes, standing strong against Pacific winds.

🌊
Island Serenity

Surrounded by grasslands, dramatic cliffs, and the deep Pacific Ocean.

🍃
Local Cuisine

Enjoy traditional Ivatan meals made with fresh local ingredients.

⭐
Personal Service

Our family attends to every guest with genuine care and local wisdom.
